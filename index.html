Twitch Polls
An easy way to integrate audience feedback into your live stream
DownloadDocs
This is how it works:

Install

Installing is as simple as downloading the RAR file above , extracting the files within, and running TwitchPolls.exe

Create Poll

After installation, to create a poll all you need to do is fill out the options presented in the application, then hit "Run Poll".

Add To Livestream

You can easily integrate the results of the poll into your live stream by adding the "Export Url" as a source in your software.
